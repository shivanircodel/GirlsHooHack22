Project Garbh

About Us

Garbh is a web app built using the Django framework. With a wealth of information about abortions and the procedures associated with them, it serves as an awareness guide. A chatbot made with Dialogflow(A Google Cloud API) has been integrated into the main page to answer all the questions related to abortion so misinformation can be avoided. Through web scraping, the website can direct the user to the nearest medical resource available to them.

Raising Awareness:

On Friday, June 24, 2022, the US Supreme Court overturn Roe v. Wade, the historic law that had declared the right to an abortion a constitutional one in the country. In the US, more than 15 states have outlawed abortion, and more than ten more are considering doing the same. Multiple studies have shown that restricting abortion may result in fewer women working and attending school. This inspired us to create Garbh, which works as a guide for women who need to seek an abortion but are unsure of how to proceed
